Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a flavorful and spicy dish that is popular in many cuisines around the world. This version is simple to make and perfect for a hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until they are soft and translucent.
  2. Add garlic and ginger to the pan, cooking for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, and cumin, cooking the spices for 1 minute to release their flavors.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir to combine and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer the curry for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Serve hot.
    8. Enjoy your delicious Chicken Curry!
